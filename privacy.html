Introduction

We take your privacy seriously.

Salmo ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that helps you rediscover the world around you through daily quests. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

By using Salmo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

01 — Data we collect

What information Salmo collects.

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app's functionality. Here is a full overview:

  • Auth Email address — collected when you sign in via email OTP. Used solely for authentication. We do not send marketing emails.
  • Profile Display name, avatar, city — entered by you during onboarding. Stored in our database and used to personalise your experience.
  • Location Precise location — collected while using the app (When In Use only) to show you nearby quests on the map. Location is never stored on our servers; it is used in-session only.
  • Activity Completed quests and personal logs — when you complete a quest or write a personal log, this data is stored in our database linked to your account.
  • Push Push notification token — stored if you grant notification permission, used only to send you relevant app notifications. Never shared with third parties.
  • Analytics Usage events — anonymous usage data (screens opened, quests completed, onboarding steps) collected via PostHog, hosted in the EU. No personal identifiers are attached to analytics events unless you are signed in, in which case your Supabase user ID is used as an anonymous identifier.

02 — How we use your data

Your data is used only to run Salmo.

We use the data we collect for the following purposes only:

— To authenticate you and maintain your account
— To show you quests relevant to your location
— To track your personal progress (levels, completed quests, logs)
— To send you push notifications you have opted in to
— To understand how the app is used so we can improve it (analytics)

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in Section 04.

03 — Data storage

Where your data lives.

Your account data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. Data is stored in the EU region. Supabase is SOC 2 compliant.

Analytics data is processed by PostHog, hosted on EU infrastructure (eu.i.posthog.com). PostHog does not receive your email address or any directly identifiable personal information.

Some data (completed quests, personal logs) is also stored locally on your device via standard iOS storage mechanisms for offline access.

04 — Third parties

Who else touches your data.

We use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

— Supabase (supabase.com) — database and authentication
— PostHog (posthog.com) — product analytics, EU-hosted
— Apple (apple.com) — Sign in with Apple authentication, push notification delivery

We do not integrate any advertising networks, data brokers, or social media tracking.

05 — Your rights (GDPR)

You are in control of your data.

As a user based in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

— Right of access — you can request a copy of all data we hold about you
— Right to rectification — you can correct inaccurate data via the Settings screen
— Right to erasure — you can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app via Settings → Delete my account
— Right to portability — you can request an export of your data by contacting us
— Right to object — you can opt out of analytics at any time by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 07.

06 — Data retention

How long we keep your data.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account via the app, all personal data associated with your account is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

Analytics data collected via PostHog is retained for 12 months and is not linked to your identity after account deletion.

Local data stored on your device is removed when you uninstall the app.

07 — Children

Salmo is not for children under 13.

Salmo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

08 — Changes

When this policy changes.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via a notice in the app or by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
